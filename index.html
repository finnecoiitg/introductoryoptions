About the Course

  • The Finance and Economics Club at IIT Guwahati is offering a three-week "Introductory Options" course that provides a comprehensive understanding of options and stock derivatives for beginners and enthusiasts.
  • Participants will have access to daily updated, curated learning resources available on the official course website.
  • Dedicated mentors will be available on a WhatsApp platform to resolve queries & assist participants in their learning journey.
